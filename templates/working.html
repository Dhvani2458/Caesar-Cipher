🔐 How Caesar Cipher Works

📌 Basic Idea

Each letter in the message is shifted by a fixed number of positions in the alphabet.


📊 Example

Original: HELLO

Shift: 3

Encrypted: KHOOR


🧮 Formula

(Character Position + Shift) mod 26


🔁 Decryption

Decryption simply reverses the shift.

KHOOR → HELLO

Shift = -3


⚡ Why it matters

Caesar Cipher is the foundation of modern cryptography concepts like substitution ciphers and symmetric encryption.
